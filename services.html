Engagement process

The process is deliberately small: understand the situation, define the scope, deliver the work, then support the handoff.

Assess

Clarify the system, the decision pressure, and what is actually broken.

Scope

Define boundaries, expected outcomes, and the right level of rigor.

Deliver

Implement or document the work with a bias toward maintainable results.

Support

Close the loop with operational guidance, follow-up, and next-step clarity.
