Meet SMITH

The Continuous Authority HUD

SMITH is the authority layer inside the Forge ecosystem.

It is not a chatbot.
It is not a personality.
It is not decorative interface design.

SMITH is a HUD — a Head-Up Display that keeps governance visible and active while you work.

It sits between intent and execution, ensuring that AI-assisted systems operate within clearly defined boundaries.

By default, SMITH remains active at all times.

What Is a HUD?

A HUD keeps critical system state visible without interrupting workflow.

In Forge systems, this means:

  • Intent is defined
  • Policy is enforced
  • Execution is monitored
  • Evidence is structured

SMITH does not generate ideas.
It validates and governs how they are carried out.

Why It Is Always Present

Governance should not be reactive.

SMITH operates continuously so that:

  • Nothing advances silently
  • Rules are applied consistently
  • Drift between expected and actual behavior can be detected
  • Decisions are recorded in structured form

Its presence creates clarity, not friction.

User Control

SMITH can be disabled in Settings.

When disabled, active governance enforcement pauses.

This is an intentional choice made by the operator.

SMITH is visible and active by default because consistent authority builds trust — but control remains with the user.

What the Symbol Represents

  • The central core represents approved intent
  • The radial structure represents controlled execution pathways
  • The symmetry represents the absence of hidden routes
  • The unified form represents a single source of authority

All execution flows from defined intent.

What SMITH Does

Brainstorm Draft Review Approve Validate Execute Compare Gate Seal

Each step is explicit.

If constraints are violated, progression stops.

This ensures that AI remains a tool — not an uncontrolled actor.

In Simple Terms

A dashboard tells you what happened.

SMITH helps ensure what happens next stays within bounds.

One Line

SMITH is the continuous HUD where intent becomes enforceable.
